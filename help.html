Voter Registration

Who is eligible to register as a voter?

Any Indian citizen who meets the following criteria is eligible to register as a voter:

  • Is 18 years of age or above on the qualifying date (1st January of the year)
  • Is a resident of the constituency where registration is sought
  • Is not disqualified under any law for the time being in force

Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) who are citizens of India are also eligible to register as voters.

What documents are required for voter registration?

The following documents are required for voter registration:

  • Proof of age (Birth certificate, School leaving certificate, etc.)
  • Proof of residence (Utility bills, Passport, Aadhaar card, etc.)
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Aadhaar number (optional but preferred)

All documents should be self-attested before submission.

How do I register as a voter on VoteIndia?

To register as a voter on VoteIndia, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Register" button on the homepage
  2. Select "Register as Voter" option
  3. Fill in your personal details, including name, age, and contact information
  4. Provide your address and ID information
  5. Upload the required documents
  6. Submit your application

After submission, your application will be verified by the Election Commission. You will receive updates on your registration status via email and SMS.

How can I check my voter registration status?

You can check your voter registration status in the following ways:

  • Log in to your VoteIndia account and check the "Registration Status" section on your dashboard
  • Use the "Track Application" feature on the homepage by entering your application reference number
  • Contact the VoteIndia helpline at 1800-111-950

The status will show whether your application is pending, approved, or rejected. If rejected, the reason for rejection will also be provided.

Can I update my voter information after registration?

Yes, you can update your voter information after registration. To do so:

  1. Log in to your VoteIndia account
  2. Go to your profile or dashboard
  3. Click on "Update Information"
  4. Select the information you want to update
  5. Provide the new details and supporting documents if required
  6. Submit your request

Common updates include change of address, correction of name or date of birth, and updating contact information. The updates will be verified before being approved.

Voting Process

How do I cast my vote on VoteIndia?

To cast your vote on VoteIndia, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your VoteIndia account during the active voting period
  2. Navigate to the "Vote" section from the main menu or dashboard
  3. Verify your identity using the two-factor authentication method
  4. Review the list of candidates for your constituency
  5. Select your preferred candidate by clicking on their name/symbol
  6. Confirm your selection on the verification screen
  7. Submit your vote

After submission, you will receive a confirmation receipt with a unique reference number. This confirms that your vote has been recorded, but does not reveal your choice to maintain ballot secrecy.

Is my vote confidential?

Yes, your vote is completely confidential. VoteIndia employs several measures to ensure ballot secrecy:

  • The system separates your identity from your voting choice
  • End-to-end encryption is used for all voting data
  • No record is maintained that can link a voter to their specific vote
  • The confirmation receipt only confirms that you voted, not who you voted for
  • Access to voting data is strictly controlled and audited

These measures ensure that no one, including election officials and system administrators, can determine how you voted.

Can I change my vote after submission?

No, you cannot change your vote after submission. Once you confirm and submit your vote, it is final and cannot be altered or withdrawn. This is similar to traditional voting systems and ensures the integrity of the electoral process.

Therefore, please review your selection carefully before confirming your vote. The system will ask you to verify your choice before final submission to minimize errors.

What if I face technical issues while voting?

If you face technical issues while voting, you can:

  • Refresh the page and try again
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try using a different browser or device
  • Contact the technical support helpline at 1800-111-950
  • Use the live chat support available on the voting page

If you were in the middle of voting and faced an issue, your vote will not be recorded until you complete the entire process and confirm your submission. You can log back in and start the voting process again.
